about me    

Joe of all trades.

For as long as I could remember, Technology has been a huge part of my upbringing:
 - The Sony Walkman handed down from my father
 - Rewinding Cassette and VHS tapes manually
 - Booting games off floppy discs and entering win in DOS to boot windows 95
 - CD and MD players, Dial-up internet (Thanks AOL!), Surviving the dreaded Y2K scare
 - Setting up my family's LAN network, fixing and building computers

All of those were significant, but what left the largest impact on me was creating my first webage at age 13;
Somehow being able to display an image on the webpage using an <img src> tag and creating my first hyperlink piqued my interest.
What cemented my love for technology was being exposed to a wealth of information and interacting with virtually anyone... in the world!

I completed my BA in Economics, and have 5+ years of experience working in project management.
In my experience interacting with numerous clients and teams from different industries, I figured out that I genuinely enjoy working with people.
And I was good at it!
My job experience, combined with my background in Math, Science, Economics and Finance has made me well-rounded - A Joe of all trades.
Still, something was missing in my life - technology.

Learning through NPower Canada has allowed me to sharpen my skills in coding, and apply collaboration skills in constantly changing environments.
I look forward to completing my certifications and to further refine my skills to develop software and work with technology to create a positive impact on society.
if my experience with technology has taught me anything, it is that change and advancement happen rapidly if we all collaborate together.
With the entire world interconnected, I would say the odds are in our favor - And I'm eagerly anticpating working with you.
